What is Hor1zon Civil War ?
Hor1zon Civil War is a battle simulation game between two teams of Troopers. Players can add their Troopers to the battle up to 1 hour before the battle starts.
The rarity of the Troopers of a team defines the power of the team. In addition to that, troopers with similar attributes increase the power.
24 hours after the start of the battle, an algorithm defines the winning team based on the strength of both teams.
How does it work ?
Example 1:
- Team 1: 10 Troopers with a total power of 1000
- Team 2: 8 Troopers with a total power of 1000

Both teams have a 50% chance of winning.

Example 2:
- Team 1: 10 Troopers with a total power of 2000
- Team 2: 15 Troopers with a total power of 1000

Team 1 has twice the chance of winning than Team 2
What is the loot of war ?
The players of the winning team win $HZN tokens.
